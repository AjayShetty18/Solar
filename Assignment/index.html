Products

Compact enclosure, wide-range power input, and embedded SIM slot for real-time data.
SDP (Solar Data Pull) devices are specialized hardware tools designed to automate and streamline solar plant data collection. These devices serve as a bridge between solar inverters, data loggers, and cloud platforms to enable real-time monitoring and analysis.
